ITI & Industrial Training Rules

Welcome to Tech Engineer Mohali. To ensure a professional learning environment and successful certification, all trainees must adhere to the following rules and regulations.

1. Attendance & Punctuality

Discipline is the key to technical expertise. We strictly monitor attendance logs.

  • Minimum Attendance: 85% attendance is mandatory to be eligible for the final certification and project submission.
  • Timings: Trainees must reach the lab 10 minutes before the scheduled batch time. Late entry beyond 15 minutes is not permitted.
  • Leave Policy: Leaves are only approved via written application signed by the instructor. Uninformed leave for more than 3 days leads to batch suspension.

2. Lab Code of Conduct

  • ID Cards: Wearing the institute ID card is mandatory within the premises.
  • Equipment Safety: Students are responsible for the system/laptop assigned to them. Any hardware damage will be penalized.
  • Mobile Phones: Phones must be on silent mode. usage of social media or gaming during lab hours is strictly prohibited.
  • Cleanliness: Food and drinks are strictly not allowed inside the computer labs.

3. Project Work & Certification

The certificate is not guaranteed; it is earned based on performance.

  • Daily Tasks: Completion of daily practical assignments is required.
  • Major Project: Submission of a Live Project at the end of the tenure is mandatory for the "Industrial Training" certificate.
  • Exit Test: A passing score of 60% in the final theory and practical exam is required.

4. Fees & Refund Policy

Please read the financial terms carefully before enrollment.

  • Registration Fee: The seat booking/registration amount is strictly Non-Refundable.
  • Tuition Fee: Remaining fees must be cleared within the first 7 days of batch commencement.
  • Dropouts: No refund will be provided if a student leaves the course midway due to personal reasons.
  • Batch Transfer: Batch timing changes are subject to availability and management approval.
